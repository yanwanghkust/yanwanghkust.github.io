  • Langmuir Circulation

    Explicit surface waves are modeled by a moving-mesh model; the domain's upper boundary undulates as waves. Langmuir number La = 0.4, wind stress 0.33N/m^2, wave amplitude 0.8m, wave length 45m, and wave period 6s. Domain size is 628m,314m,100m in the x,y,z directions. ( Youtube video )


    --------------------------------------------------

    Related papers:

  • Wang, P. , Özgökmen, T. M., 2017. Langmuir circulation with explicit surface waves from moving-mesh modelling. Geophysical Research Letters, DOI:10.1002/2017GL076009. (PDF)

  • Wave Emission from Geophysical Vortices

    Cylindrical domain with Radius = 4 and Height = 1. Homogeneous fluid density. Case rc216-5:
    1. Vertical vorticity evolutions of an initially-unstable vortex; (Youtube video)


    2. Vertical velocity evolutions of an initially-unstable vortex, demonstrating (spiral) inertial waves; (Youtube video)


    --------------------------------------------------

    Related papers:

  • Wang, P., Özgökmen, T. M., 2016. Spiral inertial waves emitted from geophysical vortices. Ocean Modelling, DOI: 10.1016/j.ocemod.2016.01.001. (PDF)

  • Material Transport within Mixed-layer Eddies

    Cylindrical domain with Radius = 2 and Height = 1. Initially, a small patch of tracer is released in the center of a vortex at mid-depth.
    1. Case rc216-5s: evolutions of passive tracer in a initially-stable vortex (Youtube video).


    2. Case rc216-5: evolutions of passive tracer in an initially-unstable vortex (Youtube video).
